A falling block puzzle game similar to Tetris.

Controls:
_ Move Left: Left Arrow, Numpad 4
_ Move Right: Right Arrow, Numpad 6
_ Rotate Clockwise: Up Arrow; X; Numpad 1, 5, and 9
_ Rotate Counterclockwise: Z, Left CTRL, Right CTRL, Numpad 3 and 7
_ Drop Current Piece: Down Arrow, Space, Numpad 2 and 8
_ Freeze Current Piece: Left Shift, Right Shift, C, Numpad 0 \* Pause Game: ESC, F1
